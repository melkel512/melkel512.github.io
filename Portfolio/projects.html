PacMan Factory Game

About the game: While this activity was called the “PacMen Exercise,” I prefer to call it the “PacMan Factory Game.” This is because the PacMan Factory will create a PacMan and put it on the screen in a random location. The PacMan will then bounce around the screen at a random speed/velocity. You can continue to add additional PacMan characters which will repeat this random speed/velocity movement/bouncing around the screen.

Bus Stop Route Animation

About the project: This “Real Time Bus Tracker” is not truly tracking a bus in actual “real time”, therefore, I prefer to call this a “Bus Stop Tracker Map Animation.” This tracker traces the route of one of the bus routes on the IUPUI Campus in downtown Indianapolis, IN, USA. The route goes around the main perimeter of campus with many stops along the route.
